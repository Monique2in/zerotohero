HTML is a mark-up language to help you make webpages!

Try something basic: you can make words bold or underlined by adding a tag around the word.

There's lots of content you can easily add to your page with tags! Such as:

  1. Headings < h1 >
  2. Paragraph text < p >
  3. Ordered lists (like this one!) < ol >
  4. Unordered lists like this < ul >:
    • I am an < li >
    • unordered list!
  5. Images < img >
  6. Links

To insert an image, you need this tag: < img src="images/horse.png" alt="Mustang" width="20" >

To insert a link, use < a href="http://hackwestern.com">Links < /a>
